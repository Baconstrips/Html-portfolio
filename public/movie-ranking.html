My Favorite Movies list by Adalid Cortes

My top 3 Favorite movies

1. Home alone

I love home alone because its a movie i can enjoy with my family. I love the story of a kid who is left home alone and has to defend his house from two burglars. I love the humor and the creativity of the kid.

2. The sandlot

I love the sandlot because its a movie about friendship and childhood. I love the story of a group of kids who play baseball and have adventures together. I love the humor and the nostalgia of the movie.

3. The Goodfellas

I love the Goodfellas because its a movie about crime and loyalty
